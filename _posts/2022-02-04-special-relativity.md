## Special Relativity

$$
\text{Special Relativity:}\\
E=\sqrt {p^{2}c^{2}+m^{2}c^{4}}\\
E=\frac{1}{\sqrt {1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} mc^{2}\\
t^{'}=\frac{t}{\sqrt {1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}}\\
m=\frac{m_{0}}{\sqrt {1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}}
\text{Relativity factor:} \hspace{1em} g=\frac{1}{\sqrt {1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}}
$$
